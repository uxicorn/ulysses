Ulysses

The UI framework for beautiful layouts.

Based on the Fibonocci sequence found in nature and the classic principles of design found in the ancient Greek Parthenon, Ulysses aspires to impress the eye of the beholder via harmonic balance between elements and typography with modular scale.

Ulysess aims to be:

  • Modular
  • Unobstrusively pluggable with Foundation & Bootstrap
  • Responsive
  • Readible
  • Search Indexable
  • F*cking Awesome!!!

Usable Utility

Ulysses is more than layout. It applies semantic meaning to common elements found all over the web.

  • Headings
  • Design patterns
  • Embelishments such as gradients and textures
